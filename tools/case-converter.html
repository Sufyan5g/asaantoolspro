About the Case Converter Tool

The Case Converter is a handy online utility that allows you to change the capitalization of your text with a single click. It's an essential tool for writers, editors, students, and programmers who need to format text quickly without manually re-typing everything.

Available Conversions:

  • UPPERCASE: Converts all letters in your text to uppercase (e.g., "hello world" becomes "HELLO WORLD").
  • lowercase: Converts all letters to lowercase (e.g., "Hello World" becomes "hello world").
  • Title Case: Capitalizes the first letter of each word (e.g., "a tale of two cities" becomes "A Tale Of Two Cities").
  • Sentence case: Capitalizes only the first letter of the first word in each sentence.
