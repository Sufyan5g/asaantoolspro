About the GPA Calculator

The GPA (Grade Point Average) Calculator is an essential tool for every college and university student. It allows you to calculate your GPA based on the grades you have received and the credit hours for each course. Keeping track of your GPA is crucial for scholarships, academic standing, and future opportunities.

How to Use Our GPA Calculator?

  1. For each course, enter the credit hours (e.g., 3).
  2. Select the grade you received for that course from the dropdown menu (e.g., A, B+, C).
  3. If you have more courses, click the "Add Course" button to add more rows.
  4. Once you have entered all your courses, click the "Calculate GPA" button.
  5. Your final GPA will be displayed in the result box.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What grading scale does this calculator use?
A: Our calculator uses a standard 4.0 grading scale (A=4.0, B=3.0, etc.). This is the most common scale used in universities worldwide.

Q: Is my data safe?
A: Yes. All calculations are performed on your device in your browser. No academic or personal data is stored by us.
