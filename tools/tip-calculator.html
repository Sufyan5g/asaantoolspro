Hassle-Free Tipping and Bill Splitting

Figuring out the right tip and splitting the bill at a restaurant can be awkward and confusing. Our Tip Calculator simplifies this process. It helps you quickly calculate the tip amount based on your desired percentage and then divides the total bill evenly among the number of people in your group.

How It Works:

  1. Enter the total bill amount.
  2. Enter the percentage you wish to tip (e.g., 10%, 15%, 20%).
  3. Enter the number of people sharing the bill.
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the tip amount, the total bill, and how much each person should pay.
