What is Base64 Encoding?

Base64 is a method to convert binary data (like images or files) into a plain text format. This is useful for transferring data over systems that are designed to handle only text. Our tool allows you to both encode your text into a Base64 string and decode a Base64 string back to its original text format.

How to Use This Tool:

  • To Encode: Type or paste your text into the box and click "Encode to Base64".
  • To Decode: Paste a Base64 string into the box and click "Decode from Base64".
