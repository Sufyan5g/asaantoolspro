How to Calculate Your Zakat Accurately?

Zakat is one of the five pillars of Islam and a mandatory charity for eligible Muslims. Calculating the exact amount can sometimes be confusing. Our Zakat Calculator simplifies this process by allowing you to enter your assets and liabilities to find out your total payable Zakat for the year.

What is Nisab?

Nisab is the minimum amount of wealth a Muslim must possess for a full year before they become eligible to pay Zakat. The Nisab threshold is equivalent to the value of 87.48 grams of gold or 612.36 grams of silver. It's best to check the current market rates for gold and silver from a reliable local source to determine the Nisab value in your currency.

How to Use This Calculator:

  1. Enter the total value of your cash, gold, silver, and other investments.
  2. Enter the total amount of any debts or liabilities you need to pay off.
  3. Click the "Calculate Zakat" button.
  4. The tool will calculate your total zakatable assets and show you the 2.5% Zakat amount you need to pay.
