Boost Your Focus with the Pomodoro Technique

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. It uses a timer to break down work into intervals, traditionally 25 minutes in length, separated by short breaks. This simple technique can have a massive impact on your productivity and focus, helping you get more done in less time.

How It Works:

  1. Choose a task you want to work on.
  2. Click the "Start" button to begin a 25-minute timer.
  3. Work on the task without any distractions until the timer rings.
  4. When the timer rings, take a short 5-minute break.
  5. After four "pomodoros," take a longer break of 15-30 minutes.
