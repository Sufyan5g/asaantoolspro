Hear Your Text Read Aloud

Our Text to Speech (TTS) converter is an accessibility tool that reads text aloud in a clear, natural-sounding voice. It's incredibly useful for people with visual impairments, individuals who prefer listening over reading, or for proofreading your own writing by hearing how it flows.

How to Use:

  1. Type or paste the text you want to convert into the text box.
  2. Click the "Speak" button.
  3. Your device's built-in speech synthesis engine will read the text aloud. Ensure your device's volume is turned up.

Benefits of TTS:

  • Accessibility: Helps users with reading difficulties or visual impairments access written content.
  • Multitasking: Listen to articles or documents while doing other tasks like driving or exercising.
  • Learning: Aids in language learning by helping with pronunciation and listening comprehension.
