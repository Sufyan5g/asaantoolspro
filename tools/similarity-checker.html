Check for Duplicate Content with Ease

Our Text Similarity Checker is a simple tool designed to compare two blocks of text and calculate their similarity percentage. This is particularly useful for students who want to ensure their work is original, teachers checking assignments, or writers verifying that their content is unique before publishing.

How is Similarity Calculated?

This tool uses a basic algorithm (Jaccard Index) that breaks down each text into a set of unique words. It then compares how many words are shared between the two sets versus the total number of unique words across both. Please note this is a simple check and not a comprehensive plagiarism detector.

How to Use:

  1. Paste the first piece of text into the left box.
  2. Paste the second piece of text into the right box.
  3. Click the "Compare Texts" button to see the similarity score.
