Simplify Your Committee (BC) Management

A "Committee" or "BC" is a popular informal saving and lending system in Pakistan and other parts of South Asia. A group of people contributes a fixed amount of money every month into a central pool, and each month, one member takes the entire pool amount. This cycle continues until every member has had their turn.

Our calculator makes it easy to manage and understand the finances of your committee.

How It Works:

  1. Enter the total number of members in the committee. This will also be the duration of the committee in months.
  2. Enter the amount each member contributes every month.
  3. Click "Calculate Schedule" to see the total amount in the pool each month and other key details.
