Understanding Your Loan with the EMI Calculator

An Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender at a specified date each calendar month. EMIs are used to pay off both interest and principal each month so that over a specified number of years, the loan is paid off in full. Our calculator helps you understand exactly how much you will need to pay each month.

How It Works

Simply enter the total loan amount, the annual interest rate, and the loan period in years. The tool will use a standard formula to calculate your monthly EMI, the total interest you'll pay, and the total amount you'll repay over the life of the loan.
