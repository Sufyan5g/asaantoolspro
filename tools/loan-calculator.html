Understanding Your Loan with the EMI Calculator

An Equated Monthly Installment (EMI) is a fixed payment amount made by a borrower to a lender each month. Our calculator helps you understand exactly how much you will need to pay so you can plan your finances better.

How to Use Our Calculator

  1. Enter the total loan amount you wish to take.
  2. Enter the annual interest rate offered by the bank.
  3. Enter the loan period in years.
  4. The tool will instantly show your monthly EMI, total interest, and total repayment amount.

To learn more about EMIs and how they are calculated, read our detailed guide: Understanding Loan EMIs.
