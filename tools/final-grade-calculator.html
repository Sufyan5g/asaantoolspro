Plan Your Success: What Score Do You Need on the Final?

Heading into your final exams, it's crucial to know exactly what you need to score to achieve your target grade for the course. This Final Grade Calculator takes the guesswork out of the equation. By entering your current grade, your desired final grade, and the weight of your final exam, this tool calculates the minimum percentage you need to achieve on your final.

How to Use This Calculator:

  1. Enter your current grade in the course (as a percentage).
  2. Enter the final grade you want to get in the course (e.g., 90 for an A).
  3. Enter the weight of the final exam (e.g., if the final is worth 20% of your total grade, enter 20).
  4. Click "Calculate" to see the required score.
