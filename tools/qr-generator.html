What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a type of barcode that can be read using a smartphone camera. It stores information such as text or URLs, allowing users to access information quickly without typing.

How to Generate a QR Code:

  1. Enter the website URL or text you want to encode.
  2. Click "Generate QR Code".
  3. Your custom QR code will be instantly generated. You can right-click (or long-press on mobile) to save the image.
