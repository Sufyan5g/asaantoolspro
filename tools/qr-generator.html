What is a QR Code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a type of two-dimensional barcode that can be read using a smartphone camera. It stores information such as text, URLs, or contact details, allowing users to access information quickly without typing.

How to Generate a QR Code?

  1. Enter the website URL, text, or any other information you want to encode into the input box.
  2. Click the "Generate QR Code" button.
  3. Your custom QR code will be instantly generated below. You can right-click (or long-press on mobile) to save the image.

Common Uses for QR Codes:

  • Sharing website links and social media profiles.
  • Providing WiFi access without sharing the password.
  • Displaying product information or restaurant menus.
  • Sharing contact details (vCard).
