Secure Password Generator

Why Use a Secure Password Generator?

In today's digital world, strong passwords are your first line of defense against unauthorized access. Using weak or repeated passwords like "123456" makes you an easy target. Our Secure Password Generator creates complex, random passwords that are extremely difficult to guess.

How to Use This Generator

  1. Use the slider to choose a password length (12 or more is recommended).
  2. Select the character types you want to include (uppercase, lowercase, etc.).
  3. Click "Generate New Password".
  4. Click the "Copy" button and paste your new password where it's needed.

For more tips on online security, read our guide on how to choose a strong password.
