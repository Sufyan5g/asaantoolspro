How Many Days Are Between Two Dates? Here's How to Find Out.

Have you ever needed to know the exact duration between two dates? Whether you're planning a project deadline, calculating your age, or figuring out how long until your next vacation, calculating the number of days between two dates is a common task. While you could count on a calendar, it's slow and prone to errors, especially over long periods.

Why is Manual Calculation Difficult?

Calculating the duration manually is tricky because not all months have the same number of days, and you also have to account for leap years (when February has 29 days). This can make a simple task surprisingly complex.
