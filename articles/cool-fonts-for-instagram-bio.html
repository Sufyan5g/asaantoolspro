Have you ever seen an Instagram profile with a cool, unique font in their bio and wondered how they did it? The secret isn't a special keyboard; it's by using special text characters from Unicode. But you don't need to be a computer expert to do it yourself!

What Are These "Fancy Fonts"?

These "fonts" are actually sets of symbols from the Unicode standard that look like letters from different alphabets. Because they are symbols, you can copy and paste them into almost any app or website, including Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook, and Twitter.
