The Secret to Great AI Results: Writing Better Prompts

Artificial Intelligence tools like ChatGPT and Midjourney are incredibly powerful, but the quality of their output is directly related to the quality of your input. This input is called a "prompt." A vague prompt will give you a vague answer. A detailed prompt will give you a brilliant one.

The Basic Formula for a Great Prompt

A good prompt usually tells the AI four key things:

  • Role: Tell the AI who it should be. "Act as a professional copywriter..."
  • Task: Tell the AI what to do. "...write a product description..."
  • Context: Give it details. "...for a new brand of coffee."
  • Format: Tell it how to present the answer. "...in a friendly and exciting tone, with 3 short paragraphs."

Example: From Bad Prompt to Good Prompt

Bad Prompt: "Write about coffee."

Good Prompt: "Act as a professional copywriter and write a product description for a new brand of coffee. The coffee is an organic dark roast from Colombia. The tone should be exciting and luxurious. Present it in three short paragraphs."
